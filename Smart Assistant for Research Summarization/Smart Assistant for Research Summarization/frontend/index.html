🌌 About NeuraDoc

NeuraDoc is an AI-powered research assistant that reads your documents and enables interactive question answering and logic-based challenges.

✨ Key Features:

  • Document Analysis: Upload PDF or TXT files for intelligent processing
  • Smart Summarization: Get concise, AI-generated summaries
  • Interactive Q&A: Ask questions and get grounded answers with references
  • Logic Challenges: Test your understanding with AI-generated questions
  • Zero Hallucination: All responses are grounded in your uploaded document

🔧 Technical Stack:

  • Frontend: HTML5, CSS3, Vanilla JavaScript
  • Backend: Python Flask with AI/ML libraries
  • AI Models: Transformers, Sentence-Transformers, spaCy
  • Document Processing: PyMuPDF for PDF parsing
